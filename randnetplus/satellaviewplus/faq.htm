Why do I get an error when trying to download games?


If you get an error when downloading games it's likely
that you didn't wait for all filesto get downloaded
in the SatData folder. You should get a successful
confirmation after all files have been downloaded.
After all files have been downloaded you may restart
the program and try to download a game again.


How do I try it if I'm on Mac/Linux?


Even with the windows only disclaimer people have tried runing
the programs with Wine. Satellaview+ does not work with Wine
and would likely not run on a virtual machiene either.
As of now there are no Mac/Linux versions. Hopefully,
one day we would port it to other operating systems.


How can I earn money in BS-X?

There are a couple ways to earn money:
-There's a type of NPC that gives you money for free.
-You can sell fish to a man that sits next to the pond.
-You can earn money form some games.
